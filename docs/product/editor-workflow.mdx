## The Iris Editing Loop

The core workflow in Iris follows these steps:

### 01 / Library: Start from your library
Upload a new video clip or pick up an existing project where you left off. Your entire collection of reels, edits, variants, and timelines are stored and accessible here.

### 02 / Select + Prompt: Draw a box, describe the change
Navigate to the desired moment in your video. Use the editor to draw a bounding box around the specific area you wish to modify. Then, describe the desired edit in plain language using a text prompt.

### 03 / Generate: Multiple takes, one decision
Based on your selection and prompt, Iris generates multiple alternate outcomes for the specified region. You can compare these options side-by-side and accept the strongest result to integrate it into your timeline.

### 04 / Agent Chat: Let the agent drive
For a more conversational approach, describe your editing goals directly in the agent chat interface. The AI agent will analyze your video, identify relevant regions, generate edits, and present the results for your review and acceptance. This allows for a higher-level, prompt-driven interaction with the editor.

### 05 / Export: From edit to export
Once your video is complete and all edits are finalized, export the final cut. Iris stitches all accepted edits together into a single, high-quality MP4 file, ready for sharing.
